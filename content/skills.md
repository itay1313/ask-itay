## Frontend Engineering

React, Next.js, TypeScript, semantic HTML, and advanced CSS architecture. Itay builds web apps and headless frontends, and has production experience with component libraries such as MUI and Ant Design contexts through design-system work. He emphasizes accessibility and clean, maintainable CSS.

## Design & UX/UI

UX/UI design in Figma: information architecture, wireframing, visual mockups, and component libraries. He designs complete products (marketing sites, web apps, and a desktop application) and is expert in Figma-to-code handoff — a subject he taught professionally at Netcraft Academy.

## Design Systems

Design systems and frontend foundations are a core specialty: design tokens, reusable component libraries in Figma and code, and handoff documentation that lets teams reuse patterns across a product. Verified design-system roles: Design System Lead at LivePerson, Design System Architect at Gravyty, and design-system creation for TEMA Connect at Image Systems.

## CMS & Site Platforms

WordPress (custom Figma-to-WordPress builds such as Tactical Troop), Webflow, Framer, and headless CMS platforms connected to Next.js/React frontends.

## Motion & 3D

Motion design and 3D as part of creative development — including a custom Spline hero animation built for the Coinmore project.

## Accessibility & CSS Craft

Accessibility, semantic HTML, and CSS architecture are listed as one of his six core service areas — he treats a11y and CSS quality as foundations, not afterthoughts.

## Tools

Figma, Framer, Webflow, WordPress, Next.js, React, TypeScript, headless CMSs, APIs, n8n, Spline, design tokens, creative code.
