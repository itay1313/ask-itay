## Coinmore

Itay co-designed the Coinmore marketing site and user console in Figma, created a bold crypto visual language, structured the logged-in experience, and built a reusable component system plus a custom Spline hero animation. Delivered in 6–7 weeks, working remotely for a China-based client.

This project is proof of web-app UX (a structured logged-in console, not just a marketing page), component-system thinking, and motion/3D capability with Spline.
