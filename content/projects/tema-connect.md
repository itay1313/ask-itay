## TEMA Connect

TEMA Connect is a desktop hub where Image Systems' customers install engines, manage licenses, browse templates, buy new apps, and contact support. Over three years (2022–2025), working remotely with the team in Linköping, Sweden, Itay owned the complete design workflow: concept, structural planning and information architecture, wireframing, visual mockups, design-system and component-library creation, and final developer handoff. The handoff documentation let developers reuse patterns across the whole product.

This is his strongest proof of enterprise product design, design-system leadership, and long-term remote collaboration with an engineering organization.
