## Who Itay Is

Itay Haephrati is a frontend developer, creative developer, and UI engineer with 13+ years of creative development experience across sites, apps, and systems. He operates under his own studio brand, Com-mando (2019–present), where he designs and builds sharp, fast websites and web apps for clients worldwide.

He works across the full spectrum between design and code: he can take a product from concept, information architecture, and Figma design through design-system definition all the way to production frontend code. This dual design + engineering profile is the core of what makes him different from a typical frontend developer.

## Roles That Fit Itay

Based on his verified experience, the roles that fit Itay best are: Design Engineer, Senior Frontend Developer, Creative Developer, UI Engineer, Design System Lead, Product Designer with engineering depth, and UX Engineer. He has held Senior Design Engineer and Design System Lead titles at real companies (Image Systems, LivePerson).

## What Makes Him Different

Most frontend developers implement designs; most designers hand off mockups. Itay does both ends and owns the handoff itself: he defines foundations and reusable components in Figma, then implements them in React, TypeScript, and CSS. He has also taught this exact workflow professionally as a lecturer at Netcraft Academy (HTML/CSS and Figma handoff). On top of that he brings motion/3D work (Spline), AI and automation experience (n8n flows, AI-assisted development), and CMS/headless experience (WordPress, Webflow, Framer, headless CMSs).
