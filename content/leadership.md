## Design System Leadership

Itay was Software Engineer & Design System Lead at LivePerson (2020–2023), leading shared component foundations across designers and engineers, and Design System Architect at Gravyty (2016–2020). Leading a design system means aligning multiple teams on tokens, components, and process — organizational leadership as much as technical work.

## Teaching & Mentoring

He was a Lecturer & Instructor at Netcraft Academy (2022–2024, Israel), teaching HTML/CSS and Figma handoff to students entering the industry. Teaching a professional curriculum is verified evidence of communication skills and mentoring ability.

## Cross-team & Remote Collaboration

He has led design workflows with distributed teams: three years working remotely with Image Systems' team in Linköping, Sweden, producing handoff documentation developers relied on; remote work with LivePerson (New York); and remote client delivery for a China-based client (Coinmore). Running his own studio, Com-mando, since 2019 also means owning scoping, client communication, and end-to-end delivery.

## What Is Not Verified

There is no verified information about Itay managing direct reports as a people manager (hiring, performance reviews). His verified leadership is design-system leadership, teaching, client leadership, and cross-team process ownership.
