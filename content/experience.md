Selected client work includes Tactical Troop (Figma-to-WordPress build), MK Architects (minimal portfolio site), Coinmore (crypto marketing site and user console), and The Mindset (Galit AI brand site with n8n automations).

## Image Systems Motion Analysis (2022–2025)

Senior Design Engineer for a Swedish motion-analysis software company, working remotely with the Linköping team for three years. He led the product design of TEMA Connect, a desktop hub where customers install engines, manage licenses, browse templates, buy apps, and contact support. He owned the complete design workflow: concept, information architecture, wireframing, visual mockups, design-system creation, and final developer handoff. The handoff documentation let developers reuse patterns across the whole product.

## LivePerson (2020–2023)

Software Engineer & Design System Lead at LivePerson, a conversational-AI company (remote/New York). He combined hands-on frontend engineering with leading design-system work — bridging designers and engineers on shared component foundations.

## Netcraft Academy (2022–2024)

Lecturer and instructor in Israel, teaching HTML/CSS and Figma-to-code handoff — evidence of both deep fundamentals and the ability to mentor and communicate technical material.

## Gravyty (2016–2020)

Web Developer, Designer & Design System Architect in Israel. An early full design + development + systems role where he architected design systems alongside building and designing web products.
